Understanding Gin

History of Gin

Gin originated in the Netherlands in the 17th century and became popular in England when William of Orange became king.

Originally used for medicinal purposes, gin evolved into the beloved spirit we know today.

How Gin is Made

Gin is created by distilling neutral grain alcohol with juniper berries and other botanicals.

The distillation process allows the flavors of the botanicals to infuse into the spirit.

Key Botanicals

While juniper is the defining botanical, gin makers use a wide variety of ingredients:

  • Coriander seeds
  • Angelica root
  • Citrus peels
  • Orris root
  • Cardamom

Getting Started with Gin

The Essential Tools

To begin your gin journey, you'll need a few basic bar tools:

  • A jigger for precise measurements
  • A cocktail shaker
  • A mixing glass
  • A bar spoon
  • A strainer
  • Quality glassware

Building Your Home Bar

Start with these essentials for crafting perfect gin cocktails:

  • 1 quality London Dry Gin
  • 1 modern botanical gin
  • Premium tonic water
  • Fresh citrus fruits (lemons, limes)
  • Simple syrup
  • Angostura bitters
  • Quality vermouth

Your First Gin & Tonic

The perfect starting point for any gin enthusiast:

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice
  2. Add 50ml of your chosen gin
  3. Top with 150ml premium tonic water
  4. Gently stir to combine
  5. Garnish with a lime wedge or botanicals

Ideal Gin Mixtures

Classic Gin & Tonic

Ingredients

  • 50ml London Dry Gin
  • 150ml Premium Tonic Water
  • Ice cubes
  • Lime wedge

Instructions

Fill a highball glass with ice. Add gin and top with tonic water. Gently stir and garnish with a lime wedge.

Negroni

Ingredients

  • 30ml Gin
  • 30ml Campari
  • 30ml Sweet Vermouth
  • Orange peel for garnish

Instructions

Add all ingredients to a mixing glass with ice. Stir well for 30 seconds. Strain into a rocks glass over ice. Garnish with an orange peel.

Tom Collins

Ingredients

  • 50ml Gin
  • 25ml Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 15ml Simple Syrup
  • Soda Water
  • Lemon slice and cherry for garnish

Instructions

Shake gin, lemon juice and syrup with ice. Strain into a Collins glass filled with ice. Top with soda water. Garnish with lemon and cherry.

Aviation Cocktail

Ingredients

  • 50ml Gin
  • 15ml Maraschino Liqueur
  • 15ml Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 5ml Crème de Violette
  • Cherry for garnish

Instructions

Shake all ingredients with ice. Strain into a chilled coupe glass. Garnish with a cherry.
