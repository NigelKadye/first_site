Gin Mixers

The perfect companions for your gin experience

Classic Indian Tonic

Best with: London Dry Gins (Gordon's, Tanqueray)

Garnish: Lime wedge, juniper berries

Recipe: 50ml gin, 150ml tonic, plenty of ice

Mediterranean Tonic

Best with: Citrus-forward gins (Bombay Sapphire)

Garnish: Lemon peel, rosemary sprig

Recipe: 50ml gin, 150ml tonic, ice, gently stir

Elderflower Tonic

Best with: Floral gins (Hendrick's, Bloom)

Garnish: Cucumber slice, edible flowers

Recipe: 50ml gin, 100ml tonic, 20ml elderflower cordial

Spiced Tonic

Best with: Spiced gins (Opihr, Whitley Neill)

Garnish: Orange slice, star anise

Recipe: 50ml gin, 150ml tonic, dash of orange bitters

Homemade Drink Recipes

Simple, delicious gin cocktails with minimal equipment

üçã Gin & Tonic Twist

Equipment: Glass, spoon

Ingredients:

  • 50ml gin
  • 150ml tonic water
  • 1/2 lemon, sliced
  • 1 tsp honey
  • Fresh basil leaves

Steps:

  1. Muddle lemon slices and basil in glass
  2. Add honey and stir until dissolved
  3. Fill glass with ice cubes
  4. Pour gin over ice
  5. Top with tonic water and stir gently

üçì Berry Gin Fizz

Equipment: Glass, fork

Ingredients:

  • 50ml gin
  • 100ml soda water
  • Handful of mixed berries
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Splash of lemon juice

Steps:

  1. Mash berries with sugar in glass
  2. Add lemon juice and stir
  3. Fill glass with crushed ice
  4. Pour gin over ice
  5. Top with soda water and stir gently

üçØ Honey Rosemary Gin

Equipment: Glass, spoon

Ingredients:

  • 50ml gin
  • 100ml sparkling water
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Lemon twist

Steps:

  1. Muddle rosemary with honey in glass
  2. Add gin and stir until honey dissolves
  3. Fill glass with ice cubes
  4. Top with sparkling water
  5. Garnish with lemon twist

Equipment: Glass, spoon

Ingredients:

  • 50ml gin
  • 50ml cold brew coffee

Steps:
